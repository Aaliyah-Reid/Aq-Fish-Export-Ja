How It All Started

Our Dream is to Bring the World of Aquatics Closer

Blue Wave was founded in 2005 by ocean enthusiasts Marie Waters and Sarah Tide, who shared a deep passion for aquatic life and a desire to connect people with the wonders of fresh waters. Their vision was to create a gateway for individuals to experience the marvels of aquatic ecosystems from the comfort of their homes, bridging the gap between humanity and the sea. Over the years, Blue Wave has grown into a symbol of innovation and dedication, offering a wide range of fish species and products that reflect the beauty and diversity of fresh waters. Built on the core values of sustainability, education, and exceptional customer care, the company is committed to protecting marine environments while fostering awareness and appreciation for aquatic life.
