Tips for a Healthy Aquarium

New to aquatics? Here are some tips to get started!

Your fish will thank you—let’s dive in!

  • Choose the right tank size for your fish to ensure they have enough space to thrive.
  • Quarantine new fish for 2-4 weeks to prevent the spread of diseases.
  • Maintain proper water temperature using a reliable aquarium heater.
  • Perform regular water changes (10-20% weekly) to keep the water clean and healthy.
  • Use a quality filter to remove waste and maintain clear water.
  • Add live plants to improve water quality and provide natural hiding spots for fish.
